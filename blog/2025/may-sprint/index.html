Location

Frankfurt I was kind of excited to revisit germany, and especially pass through Köln, my favourite european train station!

Outbound May 10, 2025

Planned time: 10h 7m planned, 11h 18m actual

Transfers: 3 planned, 4 actual

Countries: 3 (UK, BE, DE)

Distance: 920Km

Most looked forward to train: ICE to Frankfurt

Havant

Departure – 11:16
Arrival Three bridges – 12:43 Departure – 12:50
Arrival St. Pancras – 13:40

Pretty standard travel, but the weather was nice. I actually remembered to take a photo of all of the trains for once!

St. Pancras

Departure – 15:04
Arrival Bruxelles Midi – 18:05

Saw le shuttle out of the window, and enjoyed some delivious food. I’m always so tempted to steal the etched glasses, but I refrained this time. Eurostar is always a 10/10.

Bruxelles Midi

Departure – 18:54
Arrival Liège-Guillemins – 20:04

Well, I was never meant to be on this train, but my Bruxelles->Frankfurt connection was cancelled due to a stuck coupling system (apparently) so we get an extra (terrible) rail connection out to Liège to meet my original train. I think my opinion of SNCB would be improved if I had intended to be on this trian, rather than it being a packed replacement.

Liège-Guillemins

Departure – 20:10
Arrival Frankfurt Flughafen Fernbahnhof – 23:36

Finaly on the correct train, and had a lovely time of this ICE 3 unit. we absolutely love high speed trains in germany… when you don’t get the Deutsch-Bahn’ed. The frankfurt Hauptbahnhof picture was taken a day later, I include it just to show off the great architecture

Inbound May 17, 2025

Planned time: 9h 40m

Transfers: 3

Countries: 3 (DE, BE, UK)

Distance: 920Km

Most looked forward to train: Eurostar to St. Pancras

Frankfurt Flughafen Fernbahnhof

Departure – 09:49
Arrival Bruxelles Midi – 13:35

The single connection I was originally meant to take. An otherwise perfectly fine journey elevated by the other passengers very politely telling off a rowdy drunk crowd. To the five german passengers who made the first leg to Köln a blast, 11/10.

Bruxelles Midi

Departure – 14:52
Arrival St. Pancras – 15:57

We love eurostar. We love eurostar. Also got a lovely view of the tunnel portal with the 30 year anniversary decal as we entered, and a lovely meal. 10/10 as always.

St. Pancras

Departure – 16:20
Arrival East Croydon – 17:06 Departure – 17:14
Arrival Havant – 18:29

Standard train journey, I always think I’ll miss this onnection and have to wait 30 minutes. I am always wrong about this.

Rating

Trains: We love DB rolling stock, we dislike DB timetabling.solid set of trains though, 8/10.

Stations: Frankfurt Flughafen Fernbahnhof was surprisingly nice, and Frankfurt Hauptbahnhof has some lovely architecture.

Destination: Frankfurt around the hauptbahnhof was easily one of the worst locations I have ever been to. The old town and parts of the river were nicer, I would definitely reccomend you take the U-Bahn or S-Bahn from the station, rather than going on foot.
